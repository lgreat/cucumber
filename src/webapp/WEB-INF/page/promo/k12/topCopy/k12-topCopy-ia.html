You know that your kids have something special inside them. We know it too. Iowa Virtual Academy (IAVA), an online program of Clayton Ridge Community School District, and K12 ignite the minds of children like yours to bring learning and innate possibility alive. Together, we help Iowa students in grades K-6 reach their true, personal potential.

Iowa Virtual Academy and K12 give Iowa kids in grades K-6 the chance to learn in the ways that are right for them. IAVA offers:

  • The award-winning K12 curriculum
  • A high-quality, tuition-free public education
  • An individualized approach to learning
  • Support from Iowa-certified teachers
  • An active, supportive school community
  • A range of extracurricular activities
