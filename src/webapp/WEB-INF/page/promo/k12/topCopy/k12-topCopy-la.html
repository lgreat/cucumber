Louisiana Virtual Charter Academy (LAVCA) is available to Louisiana students in grades K-11 through a partnership between K12 Inc. and Community School for Apprenticeship Learning Inc. (CSAL). Founded in 1997, CSAL is a not-for-profit with a mission to provide students with real-world experiences by giving them access to alternative forms of instruction. Since its inception, CSAL has operated a small middle school and high school centered on individualized attention, integrated project-based instruction, and motivating students to do their best. LAVCA is its third charter school.

The Louisiana Virtual Charter Academy and K12 give Louisiana kids in grades K-11 the chance to learn at the pace and in ways that are right for them. LAVCA offers:

  • The exceptional, effective K12 curriculum
  • A high-quality, tuition-free public education
  • An individualized approach to learning
  • An active school community
  • Highly qualified, Louisiana-certified teachers
