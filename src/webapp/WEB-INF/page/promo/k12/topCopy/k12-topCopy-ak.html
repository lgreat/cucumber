You know that your kids have something special inside them. We know it too. The Alaska Virtual AcademySM (AKVA) and K12 ignite the minds of children like yours to bring learning and innate possibility alive. Together, we are helping Alaska students in grades K-8 reach their true, personal potential.

The Alaska Virtual Academy and K12 give Alaska kids in grades K-8 the chance to learn at the pace and in ways that are right for them. AKVA offers:

  • The award-winning K12 curriculum
  • Full-time, tuition-free online public school option
  • Support from state-certified teachers
  • An active, supportive school community
  • A range of extracurricular activities
  • The only statewide virtual public school program to offer the complete K12 experience
  • The flexibility of virtual schooling combined with public school support
