The Lawrence Virtual School (LVS) is a full-time, tuition-free* online public school serving Kansas students in kindergarten through eighth grade. Families enrolling in this public school option receive the award-winning K12 curriculum, individualized for their child; support from passionate, Kansas-licensed teachers; an active, supportive school community; and much more! K12 is the largest provider of online learning for grades K-12, with a 96 percent satisfaction rating from parents, and offers a patented methodology for online learning. Lessons are delivered online under the guidance of a qualified teacher and supported with hands-on activities and high-quality books and materials. This rich mix of content and lesson delivery accommodates a diverse range of learning styles, from children who need more of a challenge to those in need a slower pace. Developed by education experts, the mastery-based K12 curriculum has helped tens of thousands of children reach their true academic potential.

LVS is open to all students in grades K-8 who reside in the state of Kansas.

We invite you to join us at one of our informational events to learn more about our families and school and to meet our teachers and administrative staff in person.

*$97 annual materials and technology fee for all students.
