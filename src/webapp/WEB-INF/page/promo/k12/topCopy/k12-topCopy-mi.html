Michigan Virtual Charter Academy (MVCA), an online charter school authorized by Grand Valley State University, and K12 ignite the minds of children like yours to bring learning and innate possibility alive. Together, we are helping Michigan students in grades K-12 reach their true, personal potential.

The Michigan Virtual Charter Academy and K12 give Michigan kids in grades K-12 the chance to learn at the pace and in ways that are right for them. MVCA offers:

  • The exceptional, effective K12 curriculum
  • A high quality, tuition-free public education
  • An individualized approach to learning
  • An active school community
  • A proven high school program for students who have dropped out of traditional school
  • Highly qualified, Michigan-certified teachers
