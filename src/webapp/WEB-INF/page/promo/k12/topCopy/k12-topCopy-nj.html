You know that your child has something special inside them. We know it too. New Jersey Virtual Academy Charter School (NJVACS) and K12 will ignite the minds of children like yours to bring learning and innate possibility alive. Together, we will help New Jersey students in grades K-10 reach their true, personal potential.

New Jersey Virtual Academy Charter School and K12 give New Jersey students in grades K-10 the chance to learn in the ways that are right for them. NJVACS will offer:

  • The award-winning K12 curriculum
  • A high quality, tuition-free public education
  • An individualized approach to learning
  • Support from New Jersey-certified teachers
  • Opportunities for face-to-face instruction for Newark students
  • An active, supportive school community
  • A range of extracurricular activities
