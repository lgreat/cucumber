You know that your kids have something special inside them. We know it too. New Mexico Virtual Academy (NMVA) and K12 ignite the minds of children like yours to bring learning and innate possibility alive. Together, we are helping New Mexico students in grades 6-12* reach their true, personal potential.

New Mexico Virtual Academy and K12 give New Mexico kids in grades 6-12* the chance to learn in the ways that are right for them. NMVA offers:

  • The award-winning K12 curriculum
  • Full-time, tuition-free online public school option
  • Support from New Mexico-licensed teachers
  • A robust Advanced Learner Program
  • Books, materials, and loaner computer system
  • World languages for grades 6-12
  • Blended instruction at our Farmington Learning Center
  • Unique ELL program available for non-native English speakers
  • High school graduation with a diploma that meets all state requirements

*NMVA will serve students in grades 6-11 for the 2012-2013 school year, and will add 12th grade during the 2013-2014 school year.
